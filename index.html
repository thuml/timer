Welcome to Timer - Pioneering Time Series Analysis

Timer is the brainchild of a dedicated team of researchers and developers from the School of Software, at Tsinghua University. Our work is inspired by the transformative impact of large language models on natural language processing. We've taken the same approach to time series data, creating a model pre-trained on massive datasets and fine-tuning for specific tasks with remarkable few-shot ability.

  • Luxuriant knowledge base based on large-scale pre-training.
  • Diverse downstream tasks to adapt to complex industrial scenarios.
  • Mighty analytical ability and generalization ability for real-world data.

Massive High-quality Dataset

In pursuit of preeminent analytical prowess within the industry, we have meticulously constructed a vast dataset that harmoniously integrates extensive scale, superior quality, and multifaceted domains. This endeavor has endowed Timer with an expansive repository of high-caliber data, thereby conferring upon it an impressively robust generalization capability across a multitude of tasks.

One-Stop Analysis Scheme

In alignment with the formidable capabilities of our model, we have engineered a complementary suite of solutions. These solutions enable you to access the advanced analytical services of our large-scale model through a variety of methods, including online API access, platform-based deployment, and even bespoke private deployment options.
The front-end API, which is designed for online invocation, is on the cusp of being launched!
